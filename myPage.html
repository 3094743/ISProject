Why I Love the Gospel

  1. It Brings Peace
    • I know I will see my deceased loved ones again
    • When life gets super crazy, I can remind myself that a perfect creator has a perfect plan. There's no need to worry so much.
    • God speaks peace to your soul in a way you won't hear anywhere else
  2. It Brings Joy
    • Adam fell that men might be, and men are that they might have joy:))
    • I know I can be with my family forever! How amazing is that!
  3. It Gives you Purpose
    • Being given the opportunity to serve so often makes one feel more optomistic and fulfilled in life
    • I know that I have existed before this life and will continue to exist after it, so what I do does matter
    • I know about God's Plan of Salvation, and thus know my identity and potential as a divine daughter of God
